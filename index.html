Our Products

SOLID WOODEN DOORS

Solid core doors are made of a mixture of wood and synthetic wood composites. They have a heavy, solid interior and a wood veneer exterior. They provide the durability, sound insulation, and look of a solid wood door. Provide better soundproofing. They are more durable and heavier duty, for security reasons.

SEMI-SOLID WOODEN DOOR

Semi-solid doors are similar in construction to solid doors, but with a slight alteration. Rather than being solid to their core, semi-solid doors feature a composite that has been cushioned with other materials, preferably MDF. Semi-solid doors are going to be lighter, easier to install, and less expensive than solid door.

MODERN WOODEN DOOR

Wood Galleria is taking pride to launch a new & exclusive product after investing hundreds of hours in research known as Modern Wooden Doors. We rate the product over semi solid doors when it comes to structure and full face design is an edge over solid as well. It comes in three variants Premium, Modest and a budgeted variant as well known as Standard.
